About Milestone Infra

Wholesale trader of best-quality construction chemical and coating materials

Milestone Infra was established in 2012 and is a highly known organization engaged in the wholesale trading of a broad assortment of construction chemical and coating materials.

Under our range, we offer admixtures, superplasticizer, pavement tiles coating chemicals, waterproofing, and weather protection chemicals prepared from optimum quality ingredients at the vendor end.
